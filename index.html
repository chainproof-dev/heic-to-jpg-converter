Frequently Asked Questions

Is HEIC to JPG conversion safe and private?

Yes, completely safe and private. All conversion happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly. Your images never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server.

What is HEIC format and why convert to JPG?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's image format used on iPhones and iPads since iOS 11. While it offers better compression than JPG, it isn't universally supported. Converting to JPG ensures compatibility with all devices, websites, and applications.

How fast is the HEIC to JPG conversion?

Conversion is nearly instant thanks to WebAssembly technology. Most images convert in under a second, and batch conversions are processed in parallel for maximum speed.

Can I convert multiple HEIC files at once?

Yes! You can drop or select multiple HEIC files at once. They'll be converted in parallel and you can download them individually or all at once as a ZIP file.

Is there a file size limit?

No file size limits. Since all processing happens in your browser, you can convert HEIC files of any size without restrictions.
