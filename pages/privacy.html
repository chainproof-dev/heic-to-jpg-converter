Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

HEIC to JPG Converter is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your photos are personal, and we've built our tool to ensure they stay that way.

No Data Collection

Your images never leave your device. All image processing happens entirely within your web browser using WebAssembly technology. We do not:

  • Upload your images to any server
  • Store your images anywhere
  • Access your files or photos
  • Track what images you convert
  • Require user accounts or registration

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics) to understand general usage patterns. This analytics:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not track individual users
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Only provides aggregate data like page views

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. We may use essential cookies for basic website functionality only.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: For hosting and content delivery
  • Google Fonts: For typography (Outfit, Sora, JetBrains Mono)
  • cdnjs: For JavaScript libraries (JSZip)

These services may have their own privacy policies which govern their data practices.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your images, there's no image data to secure on our end. Your files remain on your device throughout the entire conversion process.

Children's Privacy

Our service is available to users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@heictojpg.pics.
