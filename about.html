What is HEIC to JPG?

HEIC to JPG is a free, fast, and completely private image converter that transforms Apple's HEIC format photos into universally compatible JPG files.

Built with modern web technologies including WebAssembly, our converter processes your images directly in your browser – nothing is ever uploaded to any server.

Why We Built This

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's default photo format on iPhones and iPads. While it offers excellent compression, it's not universally supported across all devices and platforms.

We created this tool to provide a simple, fast, and private way to convert your photos without compromising your privacy or requiring any software installation.

Key Features

  • 100% Private: All processing happens locally in your browser
  • WebAssembly Powered: Near-native performance for instant conversions
  • Batch Processing: Convert multiple files at once
  • Quality Modes: Choose between balanced or maximum quality
  • ZIP Download: Download all converted files in a single ZIP
  • No Installation: Works directly in your web browser

Technology

Our converter is built using:

  • WebAssembly for high-performance image processing
  • Web Workers for non-blocking parallel conversions
  • Modern HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Hosted on Cloudflare Pages for global edge delivery
